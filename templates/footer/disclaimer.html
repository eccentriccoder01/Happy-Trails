Medical & Health Disclaimers

Travel in mountainous regions involves physical activity and exposure to varying altitudes. Please read this section carefully before booking or traveling with Happy Trails.

Not Medical Advice

Important: Nothing on this website constitutes medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. We are a transportation service, not healthcare professionals. All health and safety information provided is for general awareness only.

Altitude & Health Considerations

  • Altitude Sickness: Some of our routes reach elevations where altitude sickness may occur. Consult your doctor before travel if you have health concerns related to altitude.
  • Physical Fitness: Mountain travel may involve walking, climbing stairs, or standing for extended periods. Assess your physical fitness level before booking.
  • Pre-existing Conditions: If you have heart conditions, respiratory issues, high blood pressure, or any medical conditions, consult your healthcare provider before mountain travel.
  • Pregnancy: Pregnant travelers should consult their doctor, especially for travel to high-altitude destinations.
  • Motion Sickness: Mountain roads have curves and altitude changes. If you're prone to motion sickness, take appropriate precautions.

Pre-Travel Checklist
