Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Effective: November 3, 2025
User: eccentriccoder01

1. Introduction

At Happy Trails Bus Service, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website, mobile applications, and services.

Happy Trails ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy rights and recognizes the importance of securing your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our platform and services, whether you're browsing our website, making bookings, or contacting our customer service.

Our Privacy Commitment
  • Transparency: We clearly explain what data we collect and why
  • Control: You have rights over your personal information
  • Security: We use industry-standard measures to protect your data
  • Compliance: We follow GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, and Indian data protection laws
  • Honesty: We never sell your personal data to third parties

By using Happy Trails services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies, please do not use our services. We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time, and will notify you of significant changes.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

Personal Information
Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, government ID details (for booking verification), and profile photo (optional).
Payment Information
Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through payment gateways), transaction history, and invoice records.
Travel Information
Booking details, travel history, route preferences, favorite destinations, seat preferences, and special accommodation requests.
Technical Information
IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, cookies, usage patterns, and analytics data.
Communication Data
Customer service interactions, feedback, reviews, survey responses, email correspondence, and chat transcripts.

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you create an account, make bookings, contact customer service, or interact with our platform
  • Automatically: Through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies when you use our website or mobile app
  • From third parties: Payment processors, social media platforms (if you connect accounts), and analytics providers
  • From public sources: Government databases for verification purposes (with your consent)
Sensitive Information

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information (such as racial/ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, or biometric data) unless explicitly required for service delivery and with your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery
To process bookings, manage reservations, provide customer support, send booking confirmations, and deliver the transportation services you've requested.
Account Management
To create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, manage your profile, and provide personalized user experiences.
Payment Processing
To process payments, prevent fraud, issue refunds, generate invoices, and maintain financial records for accounting purposes.
Communications
To send booking confirmations, service updates, promotional offers (with consent), customer surveys, and important announcements about our services.
Analytics & Improvement
To analyze usage patterns, improve our services, optimize routes, enhance user experience, and develop new features based on customer needs.
Security & Fraud Prevention
To detect and prevent fraud, protect against security threats, verify identity, monitor suspicious activity, and ensure platform safety.
Legal Compliance
To comply with legal obligations, respond to law enforcement requests, enforce our terms, resolve disputes, and protect our legal rights.
Marketing & Personalization
To send targeted marketing (with your consent), personalize recommendations, show relevant promotions, and improve customer engagement.
Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: To fulfill our contract with you (booking services)
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For marketing communications and optional data processing
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations

4. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement comprehensive measures to protect it:

Encryption
All sensitive data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256). Payment information is tokenized and never stored on our servers.
Access Control
Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data. Multi-factor authentication required for all admin accounts.
Secure Servers
Data hosted on secure servers with firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and regular security audits. Servers located in certified data centers.
Regular Backups
Automated daily backups with encrypted storage ensure data recovery in case of system failure. Backups retained for 90 days.
Staff Training
All employees receive regular security and privacy training. Confidentiality agreements signed by all staff with data access.
Security Monitoring
24/7 monitoring for suspicious activity, automated threat detection, and incident response procedures to address security events.
Your Security Responsibility

While we implement strong security measures, you also play a role in protecting your data:

  • Use strong, unique passwords and change them regularly
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your account
  • Never share your login credentials with anyone
  • Log out after using shared or public devices
  • Report suspicious activity immediately to privacy@happytrails.com

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering the breach
  • Report to relevant data protection authorities as required by law
  • Provide details about what data was affected and steps we're taking
  • Offer guidance on protecting yourself from potential harm
  • Implement additional security measures to prevent future incidents

5. Third-Party Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal for secure payment processing
  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud for data storage and hosting
  • Email Services: SendGrid, Mailchimp for transactional and marketing emails
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics, Mixpanel for usage analytics
  • Customer Support: Zendesk, Intercom for customer service tools
  • SMS Providers: Twilio for booking confirmations and notifications

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms of Service or other agreements
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others
  • Detect, prevent, or address fraud, security, or technical issues
  • Respond to law enforcement or regulatory inquiries

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or bankruptcy, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such change and your rights regarding your data.

We Never Sell Your Data

Important: Happy Trails does NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We only share data with trusted service providers who help us deliver our services, and they are contractually obligated to protect your data.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform:

Essential Cookies
Required for website functionality, authentication, security, and basic operations. Cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.
Functional Cookies
Remember your preferences, language settings, and choices to provide a personalized experience. Optional but enhance usability.
Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how visitors use our website, which pages are most popular, and where improvements are needed. Used for optimization.
Marketing Cookies
Track your activity across websites to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness. Require your consent.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through:

  • Cookie Preference Center: Visit our Cookie Policy page to manage your cookie preferences
  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings
  • Do Not Track: We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use industry opt-out tools like YourOnlineChoices.com
Cookie Lifespan

Cookies may be session-based (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (remain for a set period). Essential cookies: Session only. Functional: Up to 1 year. Analytics: Up to 2 years. Marketing: 30-90 days.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We'll provide this in a machine-readable format within 30 days.
Right to Rectification
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data. We'll update your information promptly upon verification.
Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements and legitimate business needs.
Right to Restrict Processing
You can request that we limit how we use your data while disputes about accuracy or processing are resolved.
Right to Data Portability
You can receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format and transmit it to another service provider.
Right to Object
You can object to processing of your data for direct marketing, profiling, or based on legitimate interests.
Right to Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of previous processing.
Right to Lodge a Complaint
You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe we've violated your privacy rights.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at dsar@happytrails.com or use the Privacy Dashboard button on this page. We'll respond to verified requests within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).

Identity Verification

To protect your privacy, we may need to verify your identity before processing data subject requests. We'll ask for proof of identity (government ID, account verification) to ensure we're releasing data to the right person.

8. Children's Privacy

Age Restriction

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

COPPA Compliance

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents/guardians must create accounts for minors under 13
  • We provide parents with the ability to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's data
  • We limit data collection from minors to what's necessary for service delivery
  • We do not share children's data with third parties except service providers

Minors (13-18)

For users between 13 and 18 years old:

  • May travel independently on our buses with parental/guardian consent
  • Account creation requires parental/guardian authorization
  • Parents retain the right to access and control their minor's data
  • We recommend parents review this Privacy Policy with their minor children
Report Underage Accounts

If you believe we've inadvertently collected data from a child under 13 without proper consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@happytrails.com . We will promptly investigate and delete such data if confirmed.

9. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

Account Data
Active Accounts: Retained while account is active
Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 3 years of inactivity (with notice)
Booking Records
Transaction Data: 7 years (legal/tax requirements)
Travel History: 3 years for customer service
Payment Information
Tokenized Data: Until card expires or removed
Financial Records: 7 years (regulatory compliance)
Communications
Customer Service: 2 years after resolution
Marketing Emails: Until unsubscribe or 2 years inactive
Analytics Data
Anonymized Data: Retained indefinitely for research
Personal Analytics: 2 years maximum
Security Logs
Access Logs: 90 days
Incident Reports: 7 years (legal requirements)

Data Deletion Process

When data reaches the end of its retention period or you request deletion:

  • Data is securely deleted from active systems within 30 days
  • Backups are overwritten in next backup cycle (90 days maximum)
  • Anonymized data may be retained for statistical purposes
  • Some data must be retained to comply with legal obligations
  • You'll receive confirmation once deletion is complete
Legal Retention Requirements

Certain data must be retained for specific periods to comply with legal, tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements. We cannot delete this data even upon request until the mandatory retention period expires. This includes financial records (7 years), transaction history (7 years), and certain booking records.

10. International Data Transfers

Happy Trails primarily operates in India, but some of our service providers may be located in other countries. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards:

Data Storage Locations

  • Primary Servers: India (AWS Mumbai Region)
  • Backup Servers: Singapore (AWS Singapore Region)
  • CDN: Cloudflare (Global Network)
  • Email Services: United States (SendGrid)

Transfer Safeguards

When transferring data outside India, we use the following mechanisms:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses: EU-approved data transfer agreements
  • Adequacy Decisions: Transfers to countries with adequate data protection
  • Privacy Shield (where applicable): For U.S.-based service providers
  • Encryption: All international transfers are encrypted in transit
  • Data Processing Agreements: Contracts with all third-party processors
Your Rights for International Transfers

You have the right to:

  • Request information about where your data is stored
  • Object to international transfers in certain circumstances
  • Obtain copies of transfer safeguards (SCCs, adequacy decisions)
  • Withdraw consent for transfers based on consent

For questions about international data transfers, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@happytrails.com
